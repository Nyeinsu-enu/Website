🐾 Animal Match

Fun Memory Challenge Game

🎮 Game Overview

Animal Match is a fun and relaxing memory card game where players flip cards and match adorable animal pairs. Designed for all ages, it improves focus, memory, and reaction skills.

✨ Features

  • 🧠 Memory boosting gameplay
  • 🔊 Sound Effects & Background Music
  • 📱 Fully Responsive (Mobile & PC)
  • 🎨 Smooth animations & clean design
  • ⚙️ Settings control for Music & SFX

🎯 How To Play

  1. Tap any two cards to flip them.
  2. If they match, they stay open.
  3. If not, they flip back.
  4. Match all cards to win the game!

🧠 Why Play Animal Match?

Playing Animal Match improves concentration, boosts memory skills, and provides stress relief with colorful animals and relaxing sounds.

🔊 Sound & Settings

You can control Background Music and Sound Effects (SFX) from the Settings menu in the game. Customize your experience the way you like!
