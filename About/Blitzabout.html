Animal Blitz

🎮 About The Game

Animal Blitz is a fast-paced fun web game where players dash, collect rewards, and challenge their reflexes. Built for entertainment and quick action gameplay.

✨ Features

  • ✔ Smooth Dash Gameplay
  • ✔ Fun Animal Characters
  • ✔ Coin & Crown Rewards
  • ✔ Sound & Music Effects
  • ✔ Responsive for Mobile & PC

🛠 Developer Info

Created as a fun interactive web game project. Designed with love for fun game lovers.

Version: 1.0.0

📜 How To Play

Use your controls to dash forward, avoid obstacles, and collect coins & crowns. Try to beat your highest score!
