🐾 Animal Dash

Fast • Fun • Endless Adventure

🎮 Game Overview

Animal Dash is an exciting endless runner game where you guide your favorite animal hero through obstacles and challenges. Jump, dodge, and dash your way to the highest score!

🚀 Game Features

  • ⚡ Smooth endless running gameplay
  • 🎨 Colorful graphics & animations
  • 🔊 Background Music & Sound Effects
  • 🏆 High score tracking
  • 📱 Mobile & Desktop responsive

🎯 How To Play

  1. Press jump button or spacebar to jump.
  2. Avoid obstacles and traps.
  3. Collect coins to increase your score.
  4. Survive as long as possible!

👨‍💻 About Developer

Animal Dash is part of a Fun Game Web project designed to deliver enjoyable and lightweight browser games for everyone.
